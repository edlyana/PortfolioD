StyleShop UI Kit

About

I was approached by StyleShop to design a UI Kit for their mobile furniture shop application. The app’s goal is to attract users by offering a seamless browsing experience while showcasing aesthetically pleasing furniture for their homes.

Goals

The objective was to create a comprehensive UI Kit with a variety of elements that could potentially be implemented in the final application.

Process

I began by designing the foundational elements of the kit, including the color palette, fonts, and common icons to maintain consistency throughout the app. For forms and data elements, I sized buttons differently based on the specific actions of each page. Checkboxes and text fields were also included for user input.

For grid layout components, I enhanced the navigation bar to reflect the company’s quirky and aesthetic vibe. I used round buttons for a playful feel, adding shadows and a white backdrop to create a pop-up effect when users navigate between pages. For media and interactive elements, I incorporated sliders and dropdown menus, which are essential features in modern applications.

Challenges

One challenge was deciding whether to use a shadow-border on the home page for displaying the four furniture categories. Ultimately, I chose the shadow-border as it provided a more dynamic and visually appealing design compared to a plain background, complementing the automatically changing displays.

Results

After completing the UI Kit, I utilized most of its elements to design a functional prototype of the app's main pages. The result was a cohesive and visually engaging design that aligned with the company’s brand identity and user needs.
