Animal Quiz App

About

I developed Animal Quiz App, an interactive game designed for children. The game challenges users to match animals using a dropdown menu, encouraging learning through play in a simple, engaging format.

Goals

The primary goal was to create an app that is fun, educational, and easy for children to use. To appeal to the target audience, I incorporated bright and playful colors like green to match the animal theme.

Process

I used high-quality animal images to make the game visually appealing and enjoyable. Different options were also included in the dropdown menu to ensure variety and prevent repetition, keeping the experience fresh and engaging for young users.

Challenges

One of the challenges was designing an intuitive interface suitable for young children while ensuring it remained functional and visually appealing.

Additionally, implementing dynamic dropdown options required careful consideration to maintain variety without overwhelming the users.

Results

The result was a functional and engaging quiz game that children can enjoy. The app successfully blends fun with learning, making it an excellent tool for entertainment and education.

If given the opportunity to improve the app, I would add cool facts about the animals to make the game more educational and enriching for young users.
