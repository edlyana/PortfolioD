Personal Name Card

About

I designed a personalized name card to reflect my personality and personal brand. As a young polytechnic student, I aim to showcase my creativity and fresh ideas while maintaining a professional image for potential employers.

Goals

The goal was to create a name card that balances professionalism with a touch of fun, aligning with my identity as an innovative and approachable individual.

Process

I started by creating a moodboard to guide the design. The chosen palette incorporates serious tones (blue, grey, and black) with complementary fun elements (yellow and orange). This combination achieves a minimalist design that is professional yet lively.

To ensure clarity and readability, I used the Inter font for its clean and modern appearance, avoiding extra embellishments on the lettering. I also focused on making the card easy to print while maintaining visual appeal.

Challenges

A key challenge was integrating the cloud element of the logo with the word "nur." I experimented with Illustrator’s wrapping function and eventually achieved a design that felt cohesive and visually pleasing.

Results

For the front of the card, I used a crème background to provide a neutral yet warm base, allowing the moodboard colors to stand out. The placement of diagonal circular elements emphasizes the logo and directs attention to the center, where the essential details are displayed.

The back of the card features a dark blue background that mirrors the inner color of the logo’s cloud. To maintain consistency, I used the same crème tone for the text as the front background. This cohesive design ties both sides of the card together, creating a unified and polished look that aligns with my personal branding.
