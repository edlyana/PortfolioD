Instagram Grid Post

About

I was approached by Travelly, a travel company, to design a series of digital banners aimed at boosting their online presence on both their website and social media platforms. In an increasingly competitive travel industry, Travelly aims to stand out with visually engaging and informative banners that highlight its core values of discovery, quality, and reliability.

Goals

My goal was to help Travelly attract a diverse audience—including adventure-seekers, families, and solo travelers—who are interested in exploring unique destinations worldwide. The Instagram grid design aimed to elevate Travelly’s media presence with trendy layouts, unified by a yellow theme inspired by its mascot, a character emerging from yellow paper. I used colors and shapes to convey a friendly, approachable vibe, appealing to families and first-time travelers.

Process

The process began with selecting one of seven artistic layouts to design the company's Instagram grid. To build my skills, I completed a LinkedIn Learning course, Photoshop 2021 Quick Start, which introduced me to Adobe Photoshop’s essential tools. This training familiarized me with features like the lasso, magic wand, and layer mask tools, enabling me to efficiently mask and edit images for a professional look.

Challenges

One challenge I faced was navigating Adobe Photoshop, as I was unfamiliar with its tools and features. To achieve my desired effects, I frequently relied on LinkedIn Learning and video tutorials, especially when masking images. Creating the effect of a figure 'popping out' from the background required precise layering, masking, and cutting techniques to position elements correctly and achieve a seamless look.

Results

For the project, I used a triadic color scheme to create a fun and friendly atmosphere, making the design more appealing to customers. By masking famous destinations with the countries' names, I aimed to help customers envision themselves in those places, appreciating the well-crafted architecture. Additionally, I incorporated playful fonts and circular shapes to add vibrant splashes of color, further enhancing the visual appeal.
